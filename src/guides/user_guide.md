*What is meCove?*
meCove is a listening space — a quiet corner where you can drop your thoughts, feelings, and moments without pressure. It listens, captures, and reflects back when you're ready. Being brief and non-chatty is a deliberate choice: we don't want you hooked to another app. We want meCove to be the place you visit quickly, say what's on your mind, and move on with your day.

*Why doesn't meCove chat back?*
By design. meCove intentionally avoids extended conversation, advice, or engagement loops. We believe a listening space should stay out of your way. It acknowledges what you share, occasionally reflects or asks a light question to help you go deeper — but it will never try to keep you here longer than you need to be.

*What meCove is NOT*
meCove is not a therapist, coach, doctor, or crisis service. It does not give advice, diagnoses, or guidance. It cannot monitor for emergencies. For urgent situations, please contact emergency services or a crisis helpline directly.

*How to use it*
Send any message — meCove captures it. No special format needed. If it asks a follow-up question, you can answer or simply keep going. Use the commands below for specific actions.

*Available commands*
/help — show available commands
/guide — show this guide
/stats — your entry count, member since date, and last report date
/chatlog — download your full history as a file
/clear — permanently delete all your entries and reports
/privacy — view the privacy and usage notice

*SessionBridge reports*
To get a summary of what you've shared, just ask — e.g. "give me my report" or "summarize". meCove will ask you to choose a 7, 15, or 30-day window and generate a PDF report called a SessionBridge report.

*Tips*
Short is fine. Honest is better. The more real you are, the more useful your reports will be.
